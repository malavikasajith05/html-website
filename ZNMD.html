Plot of ZNMD

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara(ZNMD) is an Indian Bollywood movie.
Friends Kabir, Imran and Arjun take a vacation in Spain before Kabir's marriage.
The trip turns into an opportunity to mend fences, heal wounds, fall in love with life and combat their worst fears.

Director: Zoya Akhtar
